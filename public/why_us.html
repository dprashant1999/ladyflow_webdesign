Our
Story

It was a hot summer afternoon, and One Girl stood by the corner of the street, waiting for the bus. Her stomach twisted in knots, her legs shaky. She had started her period unexpectedly, and there was no place nearby where she could buy pads. She could feel the discomfort growing worse, her hand awkwardly trying to adjust her underwear as she tried to cope with the situation. Anxiety washed over her, as she looked around, hoping no one noticed.

Across the street, Our Owner was sitting on a bench, scrolling through his phone. When he glanced up, he saw girl shifting uncomfortably, her expression tense. He wasn't sure what was wrong, but it struck him how vulnerable she seemed. It clicked in his mind—he realized what she might be going through. He had a sister, and he’d seen her struggle with emergencies like this before, and it always left him feeling helpless.

Suddenly, an idea sparked in his mind. What if there was a way to help women like girl who found themselves in these urgent, uncomfortable situations? A solution to avoid the awkwardness, the panic, and the lack of access to essentials.

He pulled out his phone and began jotting down ideas for an app. A platform where women could request pads in emergencies and get them delivered quickly, almost like ordering food. The app would connect users to nearby stores, ensuring that a period emergency didn't have to become a crisis. Owner felt a surge of purpose, knowing this could make a real difference. Looking back at girl, he knew he had to make this idea a reality.
